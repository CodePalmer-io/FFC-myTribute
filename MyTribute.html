Black Lives Matter

Black Lives Matter is an organized movement favoring non-violent civil disobedience in protest against alleged incidents of police brutality against
Afro-American people.

Here is a small list of individual who have lost their lives because of police brutality:
  1. Eric Garner
  2. Michael Brown
  3. Geroge Floyd
  4. Tyree Crawford
  5. Breonna Taylor
Justice can only be served through change. Defunding the police is a step forward to remove control of those who abuse it and
to restore hope and peace in the communities that needs it.
